Insurance sourcing, proposal capture & compliance

Mandatory to proceed with proposal / fulfil regulatory obligations.
Locked ON (mandatory)
Purpose
  • Collect proposal information and verify identity (KYC) for insurance sourcing
  • Share necessary information with the insurer you choose
  • Meet IRDAI, AML, fraud-prevention and audit obligations
Withdrawal impact
This processing is required to proceed with an insurance proposal. If you do not proceed, we stop active processing except what we must retain by law and for audit/fraud controls.
Details (data used, sharing, retention, processors)
Data we will use (examples)
  • Identity & KYC: name, date of birth, PAN; Aadhaar only where permitted
  • Contact: mobile, email, address
  • Proposal details: nominee information, relationship, product selection, premium/payment preference
  • Risk disclosures: occupation/income band, lifestyle and risk declarations as required by product
  • Medical declarations: only where applicable for underwriting
  • Logs: consent record (purpose/timestamp/version), submission/verification logs
  • Digital session logs: device ID/IP/login timestamps (if enabled)
Processors (acting on our instructions)
  • Insurance distribution platforms / CRM tools used by the Bank/NBFC under safeguards
  • KYC/verification partners used by the Bank/NBFC (as applicable)
  • SMS/email delivery providers for essential acknowledgements
  • Hosting/security providers supporting Bank/NBFC systems
Disclosures / shared parties
  • Selected insurer (independent Data Fiduciary) to issue and service the policy
  • Regulators / authorities where required by law (e.g., IRDAI, law enforcement)
Retention
Minimum: While the proposal is active / until completion or cancellation
Typical (industry reference): [e.g., 5–10 years after policy termination/closure — configurable]
Maximum: As required by applicable IRDAI and financial recordkeeping laws/regulators
Expiry: After statutory timelines, data is archived/anonymised/deleted as per policy. See Retention Policy.
Mandatory processing is needed for proposal sourcing and compliance; it is not bundled with optional purposes.
